# NSF DREAM Research Project
 
This site documents my research apprenticeship through the NSF DREAM (Distributed Research Apprenticeships for Master’s) program. It serves as a running record of weekly progress, experiments, and findings.
 
## About
 
I am a master's student in Computer Science at DePaul University, with research interests in computer vision, AI fairness, and image analysis.
 
This project is conducted under the mentorship of **Professor Kenny Davila Castellanos**, Assistant Professor in the School of Computing at DePaul University.
 
## Project Overview
 
This project examines potential demographic disparities in AI-generated image analysis pipelines. The work involves reviewing relevant literature, conducting controlled experiments, and evaluating results with fairness-aware metrics.
 
**Current focus areas**
 
- Literature review on AI-generated image detection
- Experimental design and dataset preparation
- Feature analysis across demographic subgroups
- Evaluation methodology
